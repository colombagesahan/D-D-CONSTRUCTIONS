About Platinum Interior Designs Pvt Ltd

At Platinum Interior Designs Pvt Ltd, we believe that true luxury is found in meticulous detail, unparalleled craftsmanship, and designs that perfectly reflect the unique aspirations of our clients. With over 35 years of expertise, we specialize in creating opulent and functional spaces that redefine modern living and commercial elegance.

From sophisticated pantry cupboards to lavish living areas and state-of-the-art TV rooms, our team of seasoned designers and skilled craftsmen are dedicated to transforming your vision into breathtaking reality. We pride ourselves on delivering projects that exceed expectations, setting new benchmarks in interior design for leading hotels, supermarkets, and exclusive residences. Our commitment to excellence ensures every project is a testament to timeless luxury and innovative design.

We merge traditional artistry with contemporary trends to deliver spaces that are not just beautiful, but also profoundly personal and enduringly functional. Experience design that transcends the ordinary and truly reflects your refined taste.

Our Exclusive Services

Bespoke Pantry Cupboard Design

Elevate your culinary space with custom-designed pantry cupboards that blend supreme functionality with sophisticated aesthetics. We create layouts that maximize storage and enhance the flow of your kitchen, using premium materials and finishes, tailored to your exact needs and style.

Luxurious Living Area Design

Transform your living room into a sanctuary of style and comfort. Our designs focus on creating harmonious spaces perfect for relaxation and entertaining, featuring elegant furniture, exquisite lighting, and bespoke decor elements that speak volumes about your taste.

State-of-the-Art TV Room Design

Experience entertainment in ultimate luxury. We design dedicated TV rooms and home theaters that offer unparalleled viewing and acoustic experiences, integrated with smart home technology and plush comfort, creating your personal cinematic escape.

Master Bedroom Suites

Indulge in the serene luxury of a custom-designed master bedroom. We focus on creating tranquil, elegant spaces that promote relaxation and comfort, complete with custom wardrobes, ambient lighting, luxurious textiles, and thoughtful layouts for ultimate repose.

Spa-Inspired Bathroom Designs

Transform your bathroom into a private spa retreat. Our designs incorporate premium fixtures, natural stone, and intelligent layouts to create a luxurious and refreshing experience, ensuring every detail contributes to a serene ambiance.

Smart Home Integration & Lighting

Integrate cutting-edge smart home technology and intelligent lighting solutions to enhance convenience, security, and ambiance, all seamlessly woven into your luxurious interior design for a truly modern living experience.

Commercial & Hospitality Interiors

We craft inspiring and highly functional interiors for businesses, from boutique hotels to corporate offices and retail spaces. Our designs enhance brand identity, optimize workflow, and create memorable experiences for clients and guests alike.

Custom Furniture & Cabinetry

Beyond design, we offer bespoke furniture and cabinetry solutions. Each piece is meticulously crafted to complement your interior, ensuring unique elegance, perfect fit, and superior quality in every detail.

Color Consultation & Material Selection

Our experts guide you through the intricate world of color palettes and material selections. We ensure a cohesive and luxurious aesthetic, choosing textures, finishes, and tones that resonate with your vision and enhance the overall design harmony.
